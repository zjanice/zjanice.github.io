Research Opportunities & Openings

I am currently recruiting graduate and undergraduate students from diverse disciplinary backgrounds: computer science, information science, health sciences, AI, NLP, game design, and interactive media. If you are interested in working with me, even if you do not fit into one of the categories mentioned above, please feel free to send me an email using the instructions below.

Please feel free to call me "Janice" (e.g., in your email, you may simply say "Dear Janice" or "Hi Janice").

  1. Your name, current degree program (if applicable), and a brief overview of any relevant coursework or research experience you have.
  2. Your resume or CV.
  3. A clear and concise summary of what interests you about my research group's work (please refer to my Publications for more information), and why you would like to be a part of it. For undergraduate and master's students, I may ask you to undertake a directed study project before considering you for a fully-funded position.

For new Ph.D students, please read about the doctoral programs at William & Mary.

Please note that I do not respond to generic emails asking for a funded assistantship or to be a part of my research group as I receive many generic emails every day. I may not be able to respond to all these emails in a timely manner though I try my best.

I sometimes write posts regarding how to pursue a Ph.D. degree effectively (e.g., time management, job application, what I asked during a Zoom interview) on social media (written in Mandarin)-- you may find these notes helpful.
