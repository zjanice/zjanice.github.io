Food Pairing

Visualization of Cuisine and Ingredients

About The Visualization

There are at least 200,000 edible ingredients and more than 40 cuisines in the world. Based upon regions, cuisines may be divided to Asian cuisines, European cuisines, cuisines of the Americas and African cuisines. In this project, we try to compare and contrast the use of food ingredients in different cuisines. What ingredients are used more frequently and more popular? What ingredients are used less but make that cuisine unique? We are also interested in exploring the ingredient combinations in different cuisines. Since we hypothesize that the cuisine similarity is associated with region and/ or country, we want to examine our hypothesis and to explore how similar between two cuisines.

Cuisine Ingredient Matrix shows the popularity of the food ingredient in different cuisines, and what ingredient goes well with. The darker, the more popular of that ingredient is used in the particular cuisine.

Cuisine Similarity Matrix demonstrates how similar between two cuisines. The darker, the higher similarity between the two cuisines.
