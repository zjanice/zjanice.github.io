CSCI 420/520. Intro to Human-Computer Interaction
Overview: This course introduces the basics of human-computer interaction design and evaluation methods and techniques, such as examining user tasks, developing scenarios and storyboards, creating prototypes, iterating on prototypes to create a final design project, and evaluating the prototype. Other topics may include computer-supported cooperative work, multimodal and cutting-edge development in human-computer interaction, and virtual reality interfaces.
