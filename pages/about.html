About

Yixuan (Janice) Zhang is a 2nd year Ph.D. student at Northeastern University, studying personal health informatics(PHI) and human/ computer interaction(HCI). I am currently working with Andrea Parker at the Wellness Technology Lab, and am co-advised by Cody Dunne at the Data Visualization Lab.
