About

I am a Ph.D. student studying Human-Centered Computing at Georgia Institute of Technology, under the advisory of Dr. Andrea Grimes Parker, . Prior to transferring to Georgia Tech, I was a Ph.D. student in Personal Health Informatics at Khoury College of Computer Sciences and the Bouve College of Health Sciences at Northeastern University. My research interest lies in the intersection of health informatics and information visualization. My research contributes to the fields of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Computer Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW), Information Visualization (VIS), and Health Informatics.
